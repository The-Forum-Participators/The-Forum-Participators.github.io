How to join

Requirements:

  • You must have 10+ posts in the forums
  • You must not be rude in the forums
  • You must be most active in at least one of the listed forums.

The listed forums are:

  • Announcements
  • New Scratchers
  • Help with Scripts
  • Show and Tell
  • Project Ideas
  • Collaborations
  • Requests
  • Questions about Scratch
  • Suggestions
  • Bugs and Glitches
  • Advanced Topics
  • Connecting to the Physical World
  • Developing Scratch Extensions
  • Open Source Projects
  • Things I'm Making and Creating
  • Things I'm Reading and Playing
  • Scratch Around the World

If you've met the requirements, please leave a comment in our Scratch Studio!
